How to Prepare?

In order to enjoy your trip to the max and in order to prevent any injuries during the hike, you are kindly asked to strictly abide by the following:

No Smoking
We strictly care about preserving our nature and about preserving other people’s health. Thus smoking is not allowed from the moment you get on the bus till we get back. Smoking is also prohibited during the breaks that we will have on our way to the hike, and during the hike.
Hiker's Profile form
(Filled in the bus)
It is very important to fill and sign the hiker’s profile form before your first hike with us. In this form we will have a full profile about you, your contact number, blood type, insurance coverage, etc...
Drinking Water
Bring at least 2 liters of water. You can also bring energy drinks.
Food
Bring food that provides energy such as dried fruits, energy chocolate bars, Dates fruits(???) , nuts, etc...
Medical Claims
It is very important to fill in the profile and to tell us about any special medical condition that you have. You should also bring any medications that you usually take in normal days or in case of personal emergencies such as blood pressure, diabetes, etc...
Smile
It is obligatory to bring your smile with you :)
Hiking Shoes
Wear comfortable hiking shoes that cover the ankle since it helps you in the hike, and prevent feet injuries. It is not recommended to wear sports shoes as it slips during your walk.
Extra Shirts
Get extra shirts with you.
Hiking Pants
Wear comfortable hiking pants that cover all your legs and that are light in weight. It is not recommended to wear Jeans since they are usually heavy and stick to your body when you sweat.
Two Shoulder Backpack
Bring a two shoulder small backpack to put all your stuff in. It is not recommended to bring a backpack that has 1 shoulder since it will be uncomfortable during the hike.
Light Windbreaker
Keep a light windbreaker in your bag just in case of strong wind.
Sweater
Keep a sweater or a jacket in your bag just in case of cold weather.
Thick Socks
Wear a pair of thick & comfortable socks.
Camera
Bring your personal camera to capture all the splendid sceneries that we will see. Take nothing but pictures ...
Sunscreen
To protect your skin, bring your sunscreen.
Sunglasses
Bring your sunglasses.
Hat
Bring your hat to avoid any sun burns.
Hiking Stick
Bring your walking sticks if you have.
